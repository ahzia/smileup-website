### **What is the core environmental problem you are solving? What evidence do you have that this problem is real?**

SmileUp addresses the gap between climate awareness and real-world action among young people globally, with an initial focus on Germany. While awareness is high, consistent engagement remains low: studies show that 59% of Gen Z feel anxious about climate change, yet less than 25% actively participate in environmental initiatives. At the same time, Gen Z spends over 4 hours daily on digital platforms, which are highly engaging but rarely connected to meaningful impact.

This results in a missed opportunity to channel attention into real-world environmental action. NGOs and community organizations also struggle to mobilize and retain young volunteers, lacking tools that match the engagement level of modern platforms.

The current situation leads to passive engagement, low participation in climate initiatives, and underutilized community impact potential.

---

### **Who is your target audience?**

The target customers are NGOs, community organizations, and youth-led initiatives that need better tools to engage and mobilize young people. They require accessible, scalable solutions to increase participation, improve volunteer retention, and increase visibility of impact.

The end users are Gen Z (14–30), who are highly active on digital platforms but lack engaging, rewarding pathways to contribute to environmental and social causes. They need simple, motivating, and socially engaging ways to take action.

SmileUp connects these two groups by providing organizations with a platform to create missions and enabling users to participate in meaningful activities in a fun, rewarding way.

---

### **What is your solution and why is it better than existing alternatives?**

SmileUp provides a gamified digital platform where users discover environmental and social impact missions through short-form video content and complete real-world actions to earn “Smiles” tokens. These tokens can be redeemed for rewards, used to support causes, or to participate in community challenges.

Unlike platforms such as LetsAct or Brightest, which focus mainly on listing volunteer opportunities with limited engagement, SmileUp integrates content, action, and rewards into a single system. Unlike donation-focused apps such as ShareTheMeal, which rely on financial contributions, SmileUp enables behavior-driven impact through everyday actions.

Our key differentiator is the Smiles token system, which creates continuous motivation and measurable engagement. This approach makes participation more accessible, scalable, and engaging, transforming passive awareness into consistent action.

---

### **Do you have proof of concept? Did you iterate on your prototype? Describe each version and what you learned.**

SmileUp’s early prototype was developed during AngelHack Warsaw 2024, where we focused on civic engagement challenges presented by the city of Warsaw. We gathered feedback from NGOs, policymakers, and community stakeholders during the hackathon and won both the civic engagement category and the overall Grand Prize.

Version 1: [*https://smileup6.vercel.app/smile*](https://smileup6.vercel.app/smile)

We developed the second version for AngelHack Global Finals in Singapore, focusing on community features, gamification, and improving the user flow. This resulted in a more complete and scalable concept, and the project achieved 2nd place globally, further validating its potential through experienced international judges.

We have since built a fully functional MVP and conducted informal validation with NGO representatives and students. The next step is to launch publicly and test real user behavior, engagement, and retention in our initial markets.

Latest MVP: [*https://smileup-impactchain.vercel.app/*](https://smileup-impactchain.vercel.app/)

### **Goal 1 Description**

Enable climate-positive actions through missions completed on the platform

### **Goal 1 Metric**

# **of metric tons of CO2 avoided through alternative offers**

### **Goal Target Number**

5

### **Goal 1 Explanation**

This goal reflects SmileUp’s core mission: converting digital engagement into real-world environmental action. Each mission (e.g., public transport use, waste reduction, volunteering, tree planting) will be mapped to estimated CO₂ savings using standard environmental benchmarks. Achieving 5 metric tons of CO₂ avoided demonstrates that user behavior is translating into measurable climate impact, even at an early stage. This metric validates that the platform is generating real environmental outcomes and supports scaling partnerships with organizations.
